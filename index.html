First project

I've made a lightweight graphics enhancment solution written completely in SASL.

It don't uses any shaders or another complicated graphics solutions. It based only on dynamic dataref changing (atmosphere scattering, fog, enviroment hue, brightness of sun and it's light "rings") by impact of simulation conditions.
